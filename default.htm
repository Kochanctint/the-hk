Trinity Health Enterprise (HK) Limited

is a healthcare panel group managing a comprehensive healthcare network service of General Practitioners and an exceptionally high portion of specialists across a range of specialties within the Primary Care network. Located in convenient areas in Hong Kong, we provide quality diagnostic, medical and surgical services.


As a Specialist-governed Healthcare panel group, Trinity offers medical services including multidisciplinary specialist consultations and we focus on day case procedures / surgery & endoscopy and inpatient hospitalization arrangements to serve private and contract clients in Hong Kong & in the surrounding regions.
