Third Party Administration (TPA) Services

To further complement our healthcare services, we introduced Third Party Administration (TPA) services. TPA services are designed to allow insurance companies and corporations to offer value-added services to their clients and staff. Through our network, Trinity expedites the hospitalization process including admission, cost management and discharge of patients. Here’s a host of benefits we offer:

    • Scheme management
    • Provider network management
    • Member Services via 24/7 Nursing Hotline
    • Claims processing and Adjudication
