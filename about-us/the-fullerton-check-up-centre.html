T.H.E. Fullerton Check-up Centre

is a dedicated check-up centre which is located in the heart of two prime shopping paradises. Our highly experienced medical teams are dedicated to providing the most professional and personal check-up services. With smooth and efficient workflows, we ensure that even the most thorough health screening can be completed within 2 hours. To provide an accurate, reliable and efficient report, all data is computerized, with a report completed by experienced professionals. Same day results for visitors can be delivered by pre-arrangement. In addition, we offer an integrated service platform by direct referral to highly experienced Specialists in town and provide a full range of other wellness modules.


With our all-rounded facilities and services, our patients can enjoy a one-stop solution service by taking pleasure in our cozy VIP lounges to maximize their privacy with personal attention, and specimen collection on-site allows for an appointment at anytime, anywhere. Our 24-hour hotline service offers support for other healthcare needs as well. Besides providing services to individuals, we also deliver corporate programs and membership for executives.
